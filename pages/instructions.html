Playing this game is easy, but try hitting Trumps head in level 5...

1. First of all, you have to have a webcam that works and a friend (you can play it solo as well). This camera is just tracking your great great moves and is not sendig data to China.

2. Please stand around 1.5 meters away from your webcam, not closer, not further. Just make sure your shoulder and below belt area are clearly visible.

Good: hands and shoulders visible, hand grabs golfstick

Bad: too close to the webcam

Bad: too far from the webcam

Attention: make sure you're room is well lit and there are no other people visible in the camera screen (see top left corner).

3. Pick a golfclub (optional but fun) or just put your two hand togheter in a fist. Then swing as if you would play golf in real life.

Attention: make sure when you swing, your hands move further than your left shoulder.

Good: hands moved past left schoulder

Bad: hands dind't move past left schoulder

4. Try to hit the body (+5 points ) or the head (+ 20 points) of the opponent. After you hit or mis the target a sound will play and then it's the other players turn.

5. The harder you swing, the faster and higher the ball will go. Keep on playing and try to reach a higher level. After level 5, the player with the most points will win. Have fun!
